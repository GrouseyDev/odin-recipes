Thai Green Chicken Curry

Description

A tasty thai green chicken curry typically served with rice

Ingredients

  • 1kg Diced Chicken Breast
  • 1 Diced Green Capsicum
  • 1 Diced Large Brown Onion
  • 2 Tsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 Tsp Minced Ginger
  • 2 Tsp Crushed Chill
  • 1 Tbsp Green Curry Paste
  • 1 Tin Coconut Milk
  • 1 Tin Diced Tomatos
  • 1 Cube Chicken Stock

Steps

  1. Have Wok on medium heat, add onion, capsicum, garlic, ginger, chilli. Stir until lightly browned (basically until you can see everything pretty well mixed together)
  2. Add Chicken, stir until chicken has lightly browned off
  3. Add Green Curry Paste, stir for ~2 minutes or until its nicely all mixed together
  4. Add both tinned cocunut milk and tomatos, stir until its mixed in evenly
  5. Break the chicken stock cube into smaller pieces and spread throughout the curry
  6. Give it a little mix
  7. Simmer on low to medium heat for 10-15 minutes. Stirring occasionally. Basically, just wanting the sauce to thicken up a little
  8. Serve with rice
